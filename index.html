How It Works

A simple weekly rhythm that builds real change.

HealthSense turns reflection into action. Each week you check in, see your foundation clearly, and commit to a small number of focused steps that move you forward.

Check in across four pillars

Answer a short, structured set of questions about your Nutrition, Training, Resilience and Recovery — it takes just a few minutes.

See your foundation

Get a clear visual of where you’re strong, where you’re stretched, and which pillar needs attention this week.

Set focused OKRs

Together we define a small set of Objectives and Key Results that are realistic for the week ahead, so you know exactly what to focus on.

Get supportive nudges

Receive timely messages that help you adapt when life changes, celebrate progress and learn from the weeks that don’t go to plan.
